In ETEC 520 we had a series of assignments that asked us to evaluate institutional approaches, preparedness for, and implementation of **e-learning**. This was one of the first courses that introduced concepts about educational technology at an institutional level. In the creation of these artifacts, it became apparent that **institutional culture** is a hard-to-define yet important consideration when considering educational technology at scale.
